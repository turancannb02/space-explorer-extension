Privacy Policy for Space Explorer Extension

Data Collection and Usage

The Space Explorer extension collects and stores the following user data:

  • Game scores
  • Game settings (e.g., speed preferences)

This data is used solely to enhance your gaming experience by allowing you to view previous scores and maintain your game settings between sessions.

Data Sharing

The Space Explorer extension does not share your data with any third parties. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.
